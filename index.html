Emotion Detection Using EEG & EMG (FYP)

Developed a non-invasive system combining EEG & EMG with CNN-LSTM deep learning. Built a custom Python GUI to visualize emotions (Valence, Arousal, Dominance).

Vision-Based Grape Disease Inspection

Built grape disease detection with OpenCV (HSV segmentation) + YOLOv8 AI. Detected Black Rot, Powdery Mildew & Bitter Rot in real-time.

ROS2 TurtleBot3 Navigation

Implemented autonomous navigation with ROS2 Nav2. Designed costmap filters and LiDAR-based mapping for obstacle avoidance.

Automated Plant Watering & Monitoring

Arduino & ESP32 IoT system monitoring soil, humidity, and light. Automated pump & fan with LCD display and ESP32 control.

IoT-Based Parkinson’s Disease Monitoring

Wearable prototype with MPU6050, EMG, flex & MAX30102 sensors. Designed 3D TPU enclosure & implemented ESP32-based data logging.

Crankshaft Design & FEA

Modeled crankshaft in SolidWorks. Performed stress, modal & thermal FEA with multiple materials.

Hydroponics Monitoring System

Built a C program to monitor pH, temp, humidity & water levels. Added automation & data logging for smart farming.

Offshore Wind Turbine Simulation

Designed LabVIEW simulation of offshore turbines. Real-time monitoring, automatic control & performance optimization.

Robotic Arm Programming (ABB)

Programmed ABB industrial robot arm for peg pick-and-place forming "7". Implemented motion commands, gripper control & path optimization.

Multifunction 2-bit ALU

Designed & implemented 2-bit ALU with arithmetic & logic ops. Built using logic gates, multiplexers, & validated via Multisim.

Wood Cutting & Sorting System

Designed mechanical system with crankshaft, saw & pneumatic cylinder. Modeled in SolidWorks & optimized for torque, load & efficiency.

Parcel Stamping & Sorting System

Developed automated stamping & sorting using PLC + conveyor. Integrated barcode scanning, solenoid valves & GUI monitoring.

Piezoelectric Energy Harvesting Circuit

Designed system converting vibrations into electricity with piezo bender. Simulated with MATLAB/Simulink for high-efficiency DC output.
